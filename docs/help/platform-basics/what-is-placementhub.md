# What is PlacementHub?

> **Section:** Platform basics  
> **Source:** help  
> **Audience:** all

PlacementHub is a campus placement and engagement platform. It connects students, college placement teams (TPOs), and employers on one system: job postings and drives, applications, interviews, offers, clarifications, calendars, and reporting.

Your experience depends on your role: students see drives and applications; employers manage hiring and campus partnerships; college admins run the placement season; super admins operate the whole tenant.
