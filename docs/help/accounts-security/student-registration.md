# Student registration (campus key)

> **Section:** Accounts & registration  
> **Source:** help  
> **Audience:** all

Students join with the campus enrollment key from the placement office — not a roll number. Keys are long opaque values; paste them exactly (spaces are usually ignored).

You will also enter department, roll number, and batch year as your college defines them. If registration fails, confirm the key is current and that your email is not already registered.
