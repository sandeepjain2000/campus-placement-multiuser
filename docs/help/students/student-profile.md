# Profile, verification, and documents

> **Section:** Students  
> **Source:** help  
> **Audience:** student

Complete My Profile with accurate academics, contact details, and links. Your college may require verification before certain placement steps; until then you may see a banner — contact your TPO if you are stuck.

Documents — upload resumes, certificates, and ID proofs. Follow any naming guidance from your institute. Replace files when you update your resume.
