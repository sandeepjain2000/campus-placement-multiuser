Who uses PlacementHub
Four roles. One ecosystem. Role-based navigation.
| Role | How they join | Home after login |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Student | College provisions master list (no self-signup) | Student dashboard |
| Alumni student | Flagged on student account | Alumni jobs browse / apply |
| College admin (TPO) | Register or super-admin onboard | College dashboard |
| Employer | Register (may need platform approval) | Employer dashboard |
| Super admin | Provisioned by platform ops | Admin dashboard |
Shared across roles where enabled: alerts, data export, feedback, and in-app help.

### Notes:

<!-- Slide number: 9 -->

Multi-tenant architecture
Every college is a tenant ù data isolation with cross-campus employers

College tenant
Students belong to one college. Academic years and season labels scope batches and reporting.

Employer (cross-tenant)
One company account can request partnerships with many colleges.

Partnerships gate
employer_approvals: pending ? approved ? rejected. Until approved, campus actions are blocked.

Visibility rows
Jobs/internships/projects need per-campus college_status before students see them.
