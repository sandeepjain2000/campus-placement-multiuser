# PlacementHub

## Product Purpose
A multi-tenant campus placement management platform connecting colleges, employers, and students across India's top engineering institutions. College admins manage placement drives, student records, and offers. Employers post jobs and run hiring drives. Students browse opportunities and track their status.

## Register
product

## Users
- **College Admins**: Placement officers at IIT/NIT/BITS-level institutions managing 50–500 students per semester. Day-use, desktop-first, professional context. Need fast status scanning and approval workflows.
- **Students**: Final-year and pre-final-year engineering students. Mobile + desktop, quick reads.
- **Employers**: HR/recruitment teams from top tech and consulting firms. Occasional use, form-heavy.
- **Super Admin**: Platform operators managing tenants, approvals, and compliance.

## Brand Tone
Professional without being stiff. Data-dense but not overwhelming. Trustworthy, institutional grade. Never cute, never startup-playful.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS dashboards with hero-metric cards (big number + small label)
- Glassmorphic "cool dark tool" aesthetics
- Colorful emoji-heavy navigation
- Bootstrap-style blue-dominant layouts
- Identical card grids repeated ad nauseam

## Strategic Principles
1. Density earns trust — show data, don't hide it behind interactions.
2. Status at a glance — color and typography must communicate state without reading labels.
3. Action surfaces should feel low-risk — destructive actions need friction, approvals need speed.
4. Mobile is secondary for admin; primary for students.
